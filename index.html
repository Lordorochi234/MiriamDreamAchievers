About Us

This is a community organization based in Homabay County , Kasipul Consituency in Kenya. It was established in 2021 to advocate for orphans, and vulnerable children, widows and youth empowerment.

Since its inception, it has engded in various programs addressing the rights of children such us education and socio-economic empowerment of youth and widows, benefiting over 200 children through Early Childhood Development, sponsorship, and mentorship.

We have established ECDE center called MIRIAMS DREAM ACADEMY for safe learning environment for orphans and vulnerable children

The organization seeks support for its ongoing programs and aims to create a conducive environment for children and less fortune families to excel in their unique abolities
